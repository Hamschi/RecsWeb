Ingredients

  • 750g chicken tighs
  • 20g scallions
  • 20g ginger
  • 1tbsp soy sauce (light)
  • 2tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1tbsp soy sauce (dark)
  • 1tsp chicken stock powder
  • 2tsp sugar
  • 1tbsp glutinous rice wine
  • 2tbsp water

Instructions

  1. Rub the chicken thighs with dark soy sauce and let it sit for 5 minutes
  2. In a bowl, mix oyster sauce, light soy sauce, chicken stock powder, sugar, and rice wine to make the sauce
  3. Slice the ginger and onion into thick slices (about 1 cm wide)
  4. Heat oil in a pan and fry the ginger
  5. After about 15 seconds, add the onions and fry until golden
  6. Pour the sauce into the pan
  7. Add the 2 tbsp water to the bowl to rinse out any remaining sauce, then pour it into the pan as well
  8. Once the sauce starts simmering, place the chicken thighs into the pan (no overlapping)
  9. Cover with a lid
  10. After 3 minutes, flip the chicken thighs and add the remaining water
  11. Cover again
  12. After 15 minutes, flip once more and simmer uncovered until the sauce thickens and tastes right
  13. Remove the ginger and onions and serve the remaining liquid as the sauce
