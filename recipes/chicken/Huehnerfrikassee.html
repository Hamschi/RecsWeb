Ingredients

  • 1tbsp butter
  • 400g chicken breast
  • 1tbsp flour
  • 1pinch salt
  • 1pinch pepper
  • 4pcs champignons (white, large)
  • 1pcs onion (white)
  • 1tbsp butter
  • 100ml white wine
  • 400ml vegetable stock
  • 200ml cream
  • salt
  • 1tbsp flour
  • 1-2splashes lemon juice
  • 2tbsp crème fraîche
  • 1tsp lemon zest (optional)

Instructions

  1. Slice the chicken breast thinly and dice the onion finely
  2. Melt 1 tbsp butter in a pan over medium heat
  3. Add the chicken and cook until there are barely any pink spots left
  4. Season the chicken with salt and pepper
  5. While frying, sprinkle 1 tbsp flour over the chicken
  6. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside, then melt the remaining butter in the pan
  7. Add the onions and sauté until translucent, then add 1 tbsp flour
  8. Deglaze with white wine and stir over high heat
  9. Gradually add the vegetable stock while stirring
  10. Meanwhile, slice the mushrooms
  11. Toward the end of the sauce-making process, add the cream and salt
  12. After a few minutes, add the mushrooms
  13. After about 1 minute, reduce the heat to medium and return the chicken to the pan
  14. Stir in the crème fraîche and lemon juice (and lemon zest if using)
  15. Continue cooking for another 1–2 minutes
