Ingredients

  • 3cloves garlic
  • 0.5pcs onion
  • 0.5pcs bellpepper
  • 1pcs squid (head)
  • 12pcs shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • 8pcs mussels
  • 80ml olive oil
  • 112g tomatoes (sieved)
  • 0.5tsp paprika powder
  • 600ml fish broth
  • 0.25tsp saffron threads (or powder)
  • 1cup short-grain rice
  • 2pcs lemon wedges
  • 1handful parsley
  • salt
  • pepper

Instructions

  1. Roughly chop the garlic, finely dice the onion, finely chop the bell pepper and parsley, and cut the squid into small squares
  2. Heat a paella pan over medium-high heat
  3. Add olive oil and warm for one minute
  4. Season the oil with salt and fry the squid for 90–120 seconds
  5. Remove the squid and set aside
  6. Sauté the onions and garlic in the pan for 1 minute
  7. Add the bell pepper
  8. After 3 minutes, pour in the crushed tomatoes and season with paprika, salt, and pepper
  9. When the tomato sauce thickens (about 2 minutes), add the squid back in and stir
  10. Add the fish broth and saffron
  11. Once boiling, add the rice evenly and stir well
  12. After 5–6 minutes, add the shrimp and mussels
  13. After 5 minutes, reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer until all the liquid is absorbed
  14. Increase the heat for 1–2 minutes
  15. Remove from heat and cover for 5 minutes
  16. Garnish with parsley and lemon wedges
