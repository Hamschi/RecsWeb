Ingredients

  • 1cup rice
  • 8cups water
  • 300g chicken breast
  • 2tsp cornstarch
  • 1tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2tbsp water
  • 0.5tbsp chicken bouillon powder
  • 2tbsp vegetable oil
  • 14g ginger (optional)
  • 1tsp salt
  • 1tsp chicken bouillon powder
  • spring onions (green part)
  • pepper

Instructions

  1. Cut the ginger into thin strips and slice the spring onions into very thin tubes
  2. Cut the chicken into very thin strips/shreds
  3. Marinate the chicken with cornstarch, oyster sauce, water, chicken bouillon powder, vegetable oil
  4. Bring the water to a boil in a pot
  5. Wash the rice until most of the starch is removed
  6. Once the water boils, add the rice evenly and stir roughly with a whisk
  7. Place the lid on the pot, leaving a small gap for steam, and wait until the water boils again
  8. Cook on medium heat for 25 minutes
  9. When the rice looks fluffy, whisk vigorously and continuously for 2–3 minutes
  10. Turn the heat to high and add the chicken gradually, stirring constantly for 30–60 seconds
  11. Continue stirring the chicken for another 1–2 minutes
  12. Add the ginger strips, salt, and chicken bouillon
  13. Stir everything for 20–30 seconds
  14. Garnish with spring onions and pepper and serve
