Ingredients

  • 500g chicken tigh
  • 2tbsp fish sauce
  • 1tsp chicken bouillon powder
  • 1pinch MSG
  • pepper
  • coriander
  • 15pcs shiitake mushroom
  • 1.5pcs onion
  • coconut oil
  • 2pcs spring onion
  • 2.5cups rice
  • coconut milk (optional)

Instructions

  1. Remove the bones from the chicken thighs and cut the meat into bite-sized pieces
  2. Marinate the meat with fish sauce, chicken bouillon, MSG, pepper, and coriander and let sit
  3. Soak the shiitake mushrooms in a cup of water for 1 hour
  4. Use the chicken bones to prepare a broth
  5. Cook the rice using the homemade broth
  6. Roughly chop the onions and mushrooms
  7. Sauté the onions in coconut oil, add the chicken, and once the meat is no longer pink, add the mushrooms and cook together
  8. Slice the spring onions into thin rings
  9. Transfer everything into the rice cooker
  10. Keep warm for 10 minutes before serving
