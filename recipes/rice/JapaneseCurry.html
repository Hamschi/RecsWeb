Ingredients

  • 300g onions
  • 1tbsp olive oil
  • 1pinch salt
  • water
  • 300g beef
  • 1tbsp butter
  • 1pinch salt
  • 2cloves garlic
  • 150g carrots
  • 200g potatoes
  • 100g Japanese curry roux
  • 90% water according to the roux package instructions
  • 10% wine bases on the water amount from the roux package
  • 2tsp instant coffee (optional)

Instructions

  1. Slice the onions, peel the carrots and potatoes and cut them in bite-sized pieces
  2. Fry the onions in olive oil until they caramelize
  3. After about 10 minutes, lower the heat and add salt and a splash of water
  4. Cook for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking. Add 1–2 tbsp water whenever onions begin to stick
  5. Onions are ready once they're dark brown like soy sauce
  6. Meanwhile, cut beef into bite-sized pieces, potatoes slightly larger, carrots roughly, and chop garlic
  7. Melt butter in a pan and sauté the garlic
  8. Add beef and season with salt
  9. Once beef browns on the outside, stir in potatoes and carrots (about 2 minutes)
  10. Add caramelized onions and mix well
  11. Deglaze with wine, then add water
  12. Bring curry to a boil
  13. Once boiling, lower heat and simmer covered for 20 minutes. Skim off any foam that forms
  14. Dissolve the curry roux into the pot and simmer uncovered for 5–10 minutes
  15. Stir in coffee powder (optional)
  16. Serve with rice
