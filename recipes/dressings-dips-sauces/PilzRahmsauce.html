Ingredients

  • 250g mushrooms
  • 1pcs onion
  • 400ml cream
  • 1tsp salt
  • 0.5tsp pepper
  • 0.5tsp paprika powder
  • oil
  • 1tbsp cornstarch
  • spring onions or chives (for garnish)
  • 800g gnocchi

Instructions

  1. Finely dice the onion and slice the mushrooms
  2. Sauté the onions until they become translucent
  3. Add the mushroom slices and sauté them as well
  4. Deglaze with the cream
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika powder
  6. Mix the cornstarch with a little cold water and add it to the sauce
  7. Let everything simmer for 10 minutes
  8. Tip: you can serve this sauce with gnocchi
