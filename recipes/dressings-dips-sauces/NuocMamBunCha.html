Ingredients

  • 300ml water
  • 2.5tbsp sugar
  • 3tbsp fish sauce
  • 0.66tbsp vinegar

Instructions

  1. Add the water, sugar, and fish sauce to a pot
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil and let the sugar and fish sauce dissolve completely
  3. Once it begins to boil, add the vinegar and let it dissolve
