Ingredients

  • 250g butter
  • 160g sugar
  • 1pck vanilla sugar
  • 1tsp salt
  • 375g flour
  • sugar (for rolling, option)

Instructions

  1. Melt the butter, brown it, and let it cool
  2. Combine the browned butter with the sugar, vanilla sugar, salt and flour and knead into a dough
  3. Shape the dough into logs about 4 cm in diameter
  4. Wrap the logs in plastic wrap and let the dough rest in the fridge for several hours, ideally overnight
  5. Optional: roll the dough logs in sugar
  6. Slice the dough into discs about 0.5 cm thick
  7. Place the slices on an ungreased baking sheet and bake at 170–180°C at top/bottom heat for 20-25 minutes until they get a solid structure
  8. Take them out of the oven and let them solidify. After taking them out they will be too soft to grab
