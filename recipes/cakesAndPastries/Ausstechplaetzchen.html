Ingredients

  • 250g butter
  • 300g sugar
  • 2pcs egg
  • 1pck vanilla sugar
  • 500g flour
  • 0.5pck baking powder
  • flour when cutting the biscuits

Instructions

  1. Combine all the ingredients to make a smooth dough
  2. Let the dough chill in the fridge for at least an hour
  3. Preheat the oven: 200°C top/bottom heat
  4. Spread flour on the surface of your working area
  5. Take a handful of dough and roll it out thinly (3-5mm) on that surface
  6. Take your biscuit cutters and cut out the motifs. Then place them on a tray with parchmant paper
  7. Bake each tray for 8-12 minutes until the biscuits are golden-brown
