Ingredients

  • 125ml water
  • 125ml milk
  • 125g butter
  • 5g salt
  • 10g salt
  • 150g flour
  • 4pcs eggs
  • 200ml cream
  • 1pcs vanilla pod
  • powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 230°C (top and bottom heat)
  2. Bring the milk, water, butter, salt, and sugar to a boil in a pot
  3. Once the butter has fully melted, sift the flour and add it to the boiling milk-water mixture
  4. Stir vigorously with a wooden spoon until the dough pulls away from the sides of the pot and a white film forms on the bottom
  5. Transfer the dough to a bowl and let it cool slightly
  6. Add the eggs one by one while mixing well with a hand mixer
  7. Transfer the dough to a piping bag and pipe small cream puffs onto a baking tray lined with parchment paper
  8. Place a tray with some water on the bottom of the oven during baking
  9. Bake on the middle rack for 5 minutes
  10. Reduce the temperature to 180°C and bake for another 30 minutes
  11. Turn off the oven and let the cream puffs cool inside the oven with the door slightly open for 15 minutes
  12. Transfer the cream puffs to a wire rack and let them cool completely
  13. Scrape the seeds from the vanilla pod
  14. Whip the cream with the vanilla seeds and powdered sugar until stiff
