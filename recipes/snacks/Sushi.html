Ingredients

  • 300g sushi rice
  • 350ml water (cold)
  • 5g kombu
  • 1tbsp sake
  • 2tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1tbsp sugar
  • 0.5tbsp salt
  • filling or toppings (e.g. cucumber, tuna, salmon, sesame,...)
  • nori sheets

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice and transfer it into a rice cooker
  2. Add the water and the kombu and leave it soak for 1 hour
  3. After that: add 1 tbsp sake and mix
  4. Start the rice cooker and cook the rice
  5. Mix the rice gently so that it comes out of it's shape. Don't scrape the bottom of the pot, since these grains are usually a bit crispy and not suitable for sushi
  6. Mix sugar, salt and vinegar in a bowl
  7. Pour the vinegar mixture into the rice and mix gently with a rice paddle while fanning
  8. Optional: spread the rice into a thin layer to help it cool quickly. It's recommended to roll sushi once the rice reaches around body temperature
  9. Use immediately or place a damp tea towel over the top to stop it from drying out (use within 2 hours)
  10. Form your sushis by using the toppings and fillings.
