Ingredients

  • potato (starchy)
  • oil
  • salt

Instructions

  1. Fold the parchment paper in half twice
  2. Fold corner to corner
  3. Then fold further until it resembles the shape of a paper airplane
  4. Trim the tip so it matches the radius of the plate you’ll use
  5. Wash the potato and slice it thinly using a mandoline slicer
  6. Place the potato slices in cold water and rinse off as much starch as possible
  7. Dry the potato slices thoroughly with a kitchen towel
  8. Brush some oil onto the parchment paper and place it on the plate
  9. Arrange as many potato slices as possible on the plate without overlapping
  10. Microwave on 800 W until the potato chip gets a brown color (don't let it burn)
  11. Flip the chips and microwave so that the other sides also get brown (optional)
  12. If some edges are still soft, microwave for a few seconds at 400 W
  13. Transfer the chips to a cool plate and sprinkle with salt
  14. Tip: The chips will become fully crispy only after cooling
