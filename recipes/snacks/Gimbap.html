Ingredients

  • 5pcs nori sheet
  • 2cups short-grain-rice
  • 2tsp sesame oil
  • 0.5tsp salt
  • 1pcs carrot (large)
  • 1pinch salt
  • 250g spinach
  • 2cloves garlic
  • 0.5tsp salt
  • 2tsp sesame oil
  • 230g ground beef
  • 2tsp soy sauce
  • 1clove garlic
  • 1pinch pepper
  • 1tsp brown sugar
  • 3pcs egg
  • 1pinch salt
  • 5pcs pickled yellow radish (5 longs strips, 1cm thick)

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice. Once done, mix in salt and sesame oil
  2. Let the rice cool until it’s no longer steaming, then cover with a lid
  3. Mince the garlic
  4. Cut the carrot into thin matchsticks and mix with a pinch of salt
  5. Blanch the spinach until soft (about 30–60 seconds)
  6. Squeeze out the excess water from the spinach
  7. Mix spinach with garlic, salt, and sesame oil
  8. Marinate the ground beef with soy sauce, garlic, pepper, and brown sugar
  9. Beat the eggs with salt
  10. Make a thin omelet and let it cool slightly
  11. Cut the omelet into thin strips
  12. Drain any liquid from the carrots
  13. Stir-fry the carrots for 40–60 seconds
  14. Remove the carrots, then cook the beef until fully browned
  15. Set the beef aside
  16. Assemble the gimbap rolls with rice, spinach, carrots, beef, omelet strips, and pickled radish. Roll tightly and slice
