Thịt lợn xào chua ngọt

Vietnampork

Active Time

100 min.

Ingredients

  • 500g pork belly
  • 0.25pcs onion
  • 1clove garlic
  • 1pcs chili
  • 1tbsp sugar
  • 1-3tsp vinegar
  • 1tsp salt
  • 2tbsp fish sauce
  • 2tbsp corn starch
  • 250ml water
  • 1tbsp sugar
  • 1-3tsp vinegar
  • 1tbsp fish sauce
  • 0.5tbsp soy sauce (dark)
  • 0.5tbsp corn starch

Instructions

  1. Cut the pork into bite-sized pieces and clean it
  2. Finely chop the onion, garlic, and chili and add them to the meat. Set a small portion aside for later
  3. Add fish sauce, salt, sugar, and vinegar, then mix well
  4. Add the cornstarch and mix until evenly coated
  5. Let the meat marinate for about 1 hour
  6. Fry the meat in a hot pan with oil. Afterwards reduce the heat to medium
  7. Once the meat is golden brown, flip it
  8. When both sides are golden, remove the meat from the pan
  9. Meanwhile, add the water to the container that held the marinated meat
  10. Add the remaining sugar, vinegar, soy sauce (dark) und fish sauce to this liquid and mix well
  11. In the remaining oil, fry the reserved chili, garlic, and onion until fragrant
  12. Pour in the prepared liquid
  13. Once it starts boiling, add the pork back to the pan
  14. The dish is done once most of the liquid has evaporated and the sauce becomes very thick
