Ingredients

  • 6pcs eggs
  • 400g pork belly
  • 1clove garlic
  • 1tsp sugar
  • 1tsp chicken stock powder
  • 1tbsp fish sauce
  • 1tsp soy sauce (dark)
  • 1tsp salt
  • 2cloves garlic
  • 0.5pcs onion
  • 2tsp sugar
  • 500-800ml coconut water (optional)
  • boiling water
  • 1pcs Thai chili

Instructions

  1. Hard-boil the eggs, then peel them
  2. Wash the pork belly
  3. Cut the pork into bite-sized pieces or large chunks
  4. In a bowl, mix salt, chicken stock powder, sugar, fish sauce, and dark soy sauce to make a marinade
  5. Finely chop the garlic and briefly fry the pork with it
  6. Pour the marinade evenly over the pork and let it marinate for at least 1 hour
  7. After marinating, finely chop the garlic and onion and bring water to a boil
  8. Add oil and sugar to a pot and heat over high heat until the sugar starts bubbling, stirring constantly
  9. Once a sweet caramel aroma develops, reduce the heat to medium-high
  10. When the sugar is bubbling, turn off the heat completely
  11. Add the onions and garlic to the caramel and toss to coat
  12. Add the pork and stir well
  13. Turn the heat back up to high
  14. Add 300-400ml boiling water to the pot and reduce the heat to medium
  15. Add the eggs and chili and let everything simmer gently for 1.5–2 hours. As soon more then 50% of the water evaporates, add more boiling water or coconut water
