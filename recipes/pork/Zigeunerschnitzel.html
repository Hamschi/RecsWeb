Ingredients

  • 2pcs schnitzels
  • egg
  • flour
  • breadcrumps
  • 2pcs bell pepper (red)
  • 200ml tomatoes (canned, diced)
  • 1pcs onion
  • 2cloves garlic
  • 3tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1tbsp honey
  • 1tbsp rose paprika
  • salt
  • pepper

Instructions

  1. Lightly pound the schnitzels and season both sides with salt and pepper
  2. Slice the onions into strips and finely chop the garlic
  3. Cut off the ends of the bell peppers and slice the remaining peppers into strips
  4. Lightly sauté the garlic in oil, and once it starts sizzling, add the onions
  5. When the onions turn translucent, add the bell peppers along with salt and pepper
  6. Once the peppers have softened, deglaze with apple cider vinegar and add the honey
  7. Add the diced tomatoes and mix everything well
  8. Meanwhile, bread the schnitzels by passing them through flour, egg, and breadcrumbs
  9. Finish by frying the schnitzels until golden brown and serve with the sauce
