Ingredients

  • 2pcs veal schnitzels (top round)
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 3pcs eggs
  • breadcrumps
  • flour
  • 1knob butter
  • oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Place the flour on a flat plate
  2. Crack the eggs into a deep plate and lightly beat them (do not whisk completely)
  3. Place the breadcrumbs on a flat plate
  4. Lightly moisten the top side of the veal schnitzels with a little water and pound them evenly with a meat mallet. Flip once during pounding
  5. Season both sides with salt and pepper
  6. Dredge the meat in flour, then dip into the egg, and finally coat with breadcrumbs
  7. Heat oil in a pan and fry the schnitzels until the breading is golden and crispy. Gently shake the pan regularly
  8. Add the butter to the oil and fry for another minute
  9. Remove the schnitzels from the pan and blot off excess oil with paper towels
