Ingredients

  • 100ml water
  • 3cloves garlic
  • 20g salt
  • 2tsp pepper (white)
  • 1g baking soda
  • 1kg ground meat (mixed beef/pork)
  • beef stock (for spraying while grilling)
  • Kaymak
  • onion (diced)
  • Lepinja bread

Instructions

  1. Pour the water into a pot
  2. Roughly chop the garlic (including the skin) and briefly bring it to a boil in the water
  3. Once the garlic water has cooled, strain it and add it to the meat together with the baking soda, pepper and salt
  4. Knead the ground meat thoroughly (about 30 minutes in a stand mixer)
  5. Cover the meat with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 24 hours
  6. Shape the meat into small sausages (a sausage stuffer was used in the video). About thumb-sized, roughly 20–25 g each
  7. Grill the ćevapčići over charcoal and spray them with beef stock at regular intervals while grilling
  8. Spread Kaymak on the Lepinja bread, add onions and 10 pieces of Ćevapčići and enjoy!
