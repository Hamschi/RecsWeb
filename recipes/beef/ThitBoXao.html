Ingredients

  • 200g beef
  • 2tbsp fish sauce
  • pepper
  • MSG
  • oil for frying
  • 1clove garlic
  • 1pcs onion (red)
  • 2tsp oyster sauce
  • 2stalks spring onion

Instructions

  1. Remove any skin or membrane from the beef
  2. Knead the fish sauce, oyster sauce, pepper, and MSG into the meat
  3. Add the oil to the meat and let it marinate for 1 hour
  4. Finely slice the spring onion
  5. Finely chop the onion and garlic and fry them in a pan until golden brown
  6. Once the onion and garlic are golden, add the beef and spring onion
  7. The dish is done once the beef is no longer red
