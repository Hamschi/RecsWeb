Ingredients

  • 5pcs spring onions
  • 3cloves garlic
  • 2.5tsp ginger (grated)
  • oil
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1pcs chili
  • 0.5tbsp sesame oil (toasted)
  • 3tbsp soy sauce (light)
  • 2tbsp sugar
  • 1tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1tsp chicken bouillon powder
  • 0.5tsp soy sauce (dark)
  • 1tbsp light corn syrum (alternatively honey)
  • 2tbsp Shaoxing wine (alternatively mirin)
  • pepper (black)
  • 5tbsp water
  • 1tbsp cornstarch

Instructions

  1. Slice the chili into thin pieces
  2. Cut the white part of the spring onion into thin slices and the green part into long tubes (about 5 cm)
  3. Finely chop the garlic and grate the ginger
  4. Mix the sauce for the meat: soy sauces, sugar, oyster sauce, chicken broth powder, mirin, pepper, water, cornstarch
  5. Fry the minced meat over high heat for about 7–8 minutes until it turns brown or dark brown
  6. Set the minced meat aside
  7. In the same pan, sauté the spring onion (white part), garlic, and chili for about 1 minute
  8. Add the sauce and cook until it thickens (about 1 minute)
  9. Add the minced meat back in and fry everything together for about 1 minute until the sauce sticks to the meat
  10. Turn off the heat and stir in the green onions
  11. Serve over rice and garnish with chili
