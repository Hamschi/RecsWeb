Ingredients

  • 450g beef (preferably flank steak)
  • 60ml water
  • 0.5tsp baking soda
  • 1pcs egg
  • 0.5tsp garlic powder
  • 0.5handful potato starch (alternatively: tapioca starch)
  • 1splash oil
  • 1pcs bell pepper (green)
  • 1pcs bell pepper (red)
  • 230ml chicken stock (alternatively water + MSG)
  • 0.3tsp salt
  • 1tsp chicken stock powder
  • 1pcs onion
  • 3tsp sugar
  • 1tsp oyster sauce
  • 1tsp soy sauce (light)
  • 0.25tsp soy sauce (dark)
  • 0.3tsp pepper
  • 0.5tsp sesame oil
  • 1tbsp potato starch (alternatively tapioca starch)
  • 60ml water

Instructions

  1. Slice the bell peppers and onion into thin strips
  2. Cut the beef into larger pieces and lightly flatten them
  3. Dissolve the baking soda in the water and add it to a bowl with the beef
  4. Let it sit for 15–30 minutes
  5. Knead the egg and garlic powder into the beef
  6. Knead in the potato starch evenly so the meat is lightly coated
  7. Knead in a splash of oil
  8. Sear the beef briefly in a hot pan (about 30 seconds; it does not need to be fully cooked)
  9. Remove the beef from the pan
  10. In the same pan, sauté the onions and bell peppers
  11. Once the onions turn translucent, deglaze with the chicken stock
  12. Add the beef back to the pan and stir well
  13. Add salt, sugar, chicken stock powder, pepper, oyster sauce, and both soy sauces and mix thoroughly
  14. Mix the potato starch with water and add it once everything comes back to a boil to thicken the sauce
