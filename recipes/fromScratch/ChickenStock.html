Ingredients

  • 500g chicken bones
  • 1l water
  • 1teaspoon salt
  • 1teaspoon chicken bouillon powder
  • 0.5piece onion
  • ginger

Instructions

  1. Bring the water to a boil
  2. Dissolve salt and the chicken bouillon powder
  3. Put the onion, ginger and the chicken bones into the water and lower the temperature of the stove
  4. Let it simmer for 30-60 minutes
