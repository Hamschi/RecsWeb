Ingredients

  • 10g agar agar
  • 100g sugar
  • 3pcs pandan leave (alternatively: pandan extract)
  • 100ml water
  • 900ml water
  • 1pinch salt

Instructions

  1. Mix the agar-agar and sugar together
  2. Cut the pandan leaves into small pieces using scissors
  3. Blend the pandan leaves with the 100 ml water and then strain the mixture
  4. Bring the 900 ml water to a boil, then add the sugar-agar mixture and the salt
  5. Let everything dissolve over medium heat
  6. Stir in the pandan liquid and mix until fully combined
  7. Remove the pot from the heat and skim off any foam
  8. Pour the liquid into a container, let it cool, and refrigerate for 1 hour
  9. Cut the jelly into your desired shape
