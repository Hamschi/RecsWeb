Ingredients

  • 2-3cloves garlic
  • 50ml oil
  • 50ml water
  • 1tsp salt
  • 500g flour
  • 350ml water (lukewarm)
  • 2tsp sugar
  • 20g yeast (fresh)
  • 1tbsp salt
  • 100ml oil
  • oil for frying
  • Toppings: sour cream, cheese (cheddar, gouda),...

Instructions

  1. Crush the garlic into a glass
  2. Add water, oil, and salt and mix well
  3. Place the glass in the refrigerator
  4. Pour the lukewarm water into a bowl
  5. Dissolve the sugar in it
  6. Then dissolve the yeast in it
  7. Sift the flour into a large bowl
  8. Mix the flour with the salt, then add the yeast-water mixture
  9. Knead everything into a dough for about 10 minutes until smooth and elastic and no longer sticking to the bowl
  10. Cover the dough and let it rise for 20 minutes
  11. Pour oil around the dough
  12. Stretch the dough so the oil gets underneath
  13. Cover again and let rest for 5–8 minutes
  14. Take a handful of dough and shape it into a ball (should yield about 6 pieces)
  15. Let the dough balls rest for another 10 minutes
  16. Heat oil very hot
  17. Take a dough ball and stretch it into a flat, round shape. The edge should be thicker than the center
  18. Carefully place the dough into the oil
  19. When golden brown, flip and fry for about 1 minute
  20. Repeat with the remaining dough balls
  21. Brush the Langos with the garlic oil, spread 2–3 tbsp sour cream on top, and sprinkle with cheese
