Ingredients

  • 200g glutinous rice flour
  • 0.5tsp salt
  • 100ml water (boiled)
  • 0.66tbsp vegetable oil
  • 60ml water
  • giò

Instructions

  1. Mix salt and glutinous rice flour in a bowl
  2. Add the boiling water and stir to combine
  3. Add the oil and lukewarm water, then mix everything evenly
  4. Knead the dough until it becomes smooth, soft, and stable
  5. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and let it rest for 1 hour
  6. After resting, cut a piece of parchment paper into a round shape so that it fits inside the steamer, and place it there
  7. Brush the parchment paper with oil
  8. Divide the dough into 8 equal pieces, about 43 g each
  9. Shape each piece into a round disk about 2 cm thick and 5 cm in diameter
  10. Place the dough pieces on the oiled parchment paper in the steamer
  11. Cover with the lid and steam for 10–12 minutes
  12. After steaming, brush each piece with oil to prevent drying or sticking
  13. Cover again with the lid and let them rest for 10 minutes (do not steam)
  14. Separate the pieces with green leaves — possibly pandan leaves — to prevent sticking
  15. Serve with a similarly thick slice of giò
