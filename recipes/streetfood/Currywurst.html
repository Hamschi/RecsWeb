Ingredients

  • 2pcs bratwurst
  • 0.5pcs bell pepper (red)
  • 1pcs onion (white)
  • 250ml orange juice
  • 100ml cola
  • 650g ketchup
  • 2tbsp curry powder

Instructions

  1. Cut the onion and bell pepper into small cubes
  2. In a pot, combine the onion, bell pepper, orange juice, and cola
  3. Bring the ingredients in the pot to a boil over high heat until the sauce becomes thick
  4. Once it reaches a syrup-like consistency, add ketchup and curry powder and stir well
  5. Let the sauce simmer for about 2 minutes
  6. Make shallow cuts along one side of each sausage at regular intervals
  7. Fry or grill the sausages
  8. Serve the sausages on a plate and pour the sauce over them
