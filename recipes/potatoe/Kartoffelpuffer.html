Ingredients

  • 500g potatoes (waxy)
  • 0.5pcs onion
  • nutmeg
  • salt
  • pepper
  • oil

Instructions

  1. Grate the potatoes and onion into a bowl using a coarse grater
  2. Place the grated mixture into a sieve and press out the liquid, collecting the liquid in a separate container. Leave the container untouched so the starch can settle
  3. Return the grated mixture to the bowl
  4. Add the egg, salt, pepper, and nutmeg and mix everything well
  5. Once the starch has settled at the bottom of the container, pour off the water
  6. Add the potato mixture to the starch and mix thoroughly
  7. Heat oil in a pan and add portions of the potato mixture
  8. As soon as they are in the pan, lower the heat so they cook slowly and evenly until they form a crust (about 5 minutes)
  9. When the pancakes are golden and crispy, flip them
  10. Once done, place them on paper towels to absorb excess oil
