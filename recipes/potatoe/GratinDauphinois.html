Ingredients

  • 1.5kg potatoes (waxy)
  • 500ml cream
  • 2sprigs rosemary
  • 2cloves garlic
  • salt
  • pepper
  • nutmeg
  • Butter for greasing the baking dish
  • 50g Parmesan

Instructions

  1. Peel the potatoes
  2. Crush the garlic cloves with the flat side of a knife
  3. Combine cream, garlic, rosemary, salt, pepper, and nutmeg in a pot
  4. Bring the cream to a boil. It should be salty and flavorful
  5. Slice the potatoes into thin, even slices
  6. Grease the baking dish with butter
  7. Add a layer of potatoes to the baking dish
  8. Pour some of the cream mixture over this layer
  9. Optionally season with salt and pepper between layers
  10. Grate a layer of Parmesan on top
  11. Repeat the last four steps until all ingredients are used up
  12. Cover the dish (e.g., with aluminum foil) and place it in the oven
  13. Set the oven to 180°C
  14. Once the cream starts bubbling (about 45 minutes), remove the cover and continue baking until the gratin is golden brown
  15. Leave it in the oven for another 30 minutes
