Ingredients

  • 3tbsp Thai Tea Mix
  • 1cup water
  • 1.5tbsp sweetened condensed milk
  • 2tsp sugar
  • 1pinch salt
  • ice
  • 2tbsp evaporated milk (unsweetened)

Instructions

  1. Place a clean kitchen towel (or paper towel) over a small to medium container and add the tea leaves
  2. Pour the boiling water through the cloth and let the tea steep for 3–5 minutes
  3. Add the sweetened condensed milk, sugar, salt, and brewed tea to a glass and stir well. Important: Taste at this stage. The tea should be slightly sweeter than ideal, as the ice will dilute it
  4. Pour the tea over a glass filled with ice
  5. Top with the evaporated milk
