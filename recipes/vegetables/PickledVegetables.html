Ingredients

  • 100g carrots
  • 100g kohlrabi
  • 1tsp salt
  • 2tbsp sugar
  • 2tbsp vinegar
  • 4tbsp water

Instructions

  1. Peel and cut the kohlrabi and carrots into your desired shape. For Bun Cha, slice into pieces about 1.5 × 2 cm and 1–2 mm thick
  2. Place the vegetables in separate bowls
  3. Mix in 0.5 teaspoon salt per bowl and let them sit for 30 minutes.
  4. After resting, gently squeeze the vegetables to remove any liquid and discard the drained liquid
  5. Combine all vegetables in one bowl, then add sugar, vinegar, and water. Mix thoroughly
  6. Let everything sit and marinate for 2–3 hours
