Ingredients

  • 400g spinach leaves
  • 2pcs shallots
  • 1clove garlic
  • butter
  • 500ml cream
  • salt
  • pepper
  • nutmeg

Instructions

  1. Prepare a bowl of ice water to cool the spinach after blanching
  2. Heat water in a pot and briefly blanch the spinach
  3. Once the spinach has wilted, drain it well and immediately place it into the ice water
  4. Drain the cooled spinach, wrap it in a kitchen towel, and squeeze out the excess water thoroughly
  5. Finely dice the shallots and garlic
  6. Sauté the shallots and garlic in butter until soft and translucent
  7. When they are soft, add the cream
  8. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg
  9. Once the cream begins to boil, reduce the heat and let it simmer until slightly thickened
  10. Meanwhile, finely chop the spinach
  11. When the cream has thickened, add the chopped spinach and mix until well combined
