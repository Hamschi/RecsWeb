Ingredients

  • 1pcs cucumber
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1tsp sugar
  • 0.5pcs onion
  • 1tbsp dill
  • 2tbsp apple vinegar
  • 1tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Peel the cucumber lengthwise from top to bottom
  2. Cut the onion into small cubes
  3. Remove the dill stems and finely chop the leaves
  4. Shave or slice the cucumbers into a bowl and add salt, pepper, and sugar
  5. Stir everything together and let it sit for 10 minutes
  6. Add the onions and dill to the cucumbers and mix well
  7. Stir in apple cider vinegar and vegetable oil, then let it sit for another 10 minutes
  8. Serve the cucumber salad without the dressing
