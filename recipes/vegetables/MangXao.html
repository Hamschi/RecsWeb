Ingredients

  • 1kg bamboo shoots
  • 7-8tbs fish sauce
  • spring onions

Instructions

  1. If not precut: cut the bamboo shoots into bite-sized pieces
  2. Put the bamboo shoots into a pot and cover them with water
  3. Bring the water to a boil and let the bamboo shoots boil for 10 minutes
  4. Meanwhile cut the spring onions in 1cm-tubes
  5. Pour the water away and cover the bamboo shoots in fresh water
  6. Again bring it to a boil and let it boil for 10 minutes
  7. Pour the water away
  8. Heat oil in a pan and add the bamboo shoots to panfry them. You may have to divide the bamboo shoots into multiple batches depending on the size of your pan and the amount of bamboo shoots
  9. Add the fish sauce to your bamboo shoots and mix it up
  10. As soon as the bamboo shoots get soft, add the spring onions and cook for one more minute
