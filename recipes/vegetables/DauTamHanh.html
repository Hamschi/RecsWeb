Ingredients

  • 2blocks tofu
  • 1bunch spring onions (ca. 500g)
  • 4-6tbsp fish sauce
  • 1tsp sugar
  • 1tsp MSG
  • oil (for frying)
  • 120ml warm/hot water

Instructions

  1. Line a large plate with paper towels
  2. Cut the tofu into your desired shape and place it on the paper towels
  3. Slice the green part of the spring onions as thinly as possible
  4. Dissolve MSG and sugar in the warm water
  5. Stir in the fish sauce
  6. Add the spring onions and mix well
  7. Once the tofu stops releasing water, deep-fry it
  8. When the tofu is cooked and crispy on all sides, remove it and briefly dip it into the fish sauce mixture
