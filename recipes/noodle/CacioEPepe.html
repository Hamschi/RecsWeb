Ingredients

  • 300g spaghettoni
  • 200g Pecorino Romano
  • pepper
  • rock salt
  • 3l water

Instructions

  1. Bring the water to a boil and add the salt
  2. Cook the pasta until al dente
  3. Grind a generous, even layer of pepper onto the surface of a warm pan (medium heat) for a few minutes
  4. Once the pepper aroma becomes strong, add 2/3 of a ladle of pasta water to the pepper
  5. Grate the cheese into a bowl and add 1/4 ladle of pasta water, mixing until it forms a paste-like mass
  6. Transfer the pasta to the pan with the pepper
  7. Add one ladle of pasta water to the noodles and stir evenly. Add more pasta water if too dry (the pasta must continue cooking in it)
  8. Add additional pepper over the pasta
  9. Let the noodles finish cooking in the pan
  10. Turn off the heat and add the cheese paste to the pan
  11. Mix everything until it forms a creamy sauce and avoid clumps. The cook in the original video uses pan-tossing
  12. Serve the pasta topped with more cheese and pepper
