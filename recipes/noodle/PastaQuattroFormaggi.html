Ingredients

  • 50g gorgonzola (alternatively blue cheese)
  • 50g stracchino (alternatively ricotta or cottage cheese)
  • 50g mascarpone
  • 4tbsp Pecorino Romano
  • 200g penne
  • 80ml whole milk
  • 1tbsp salt
  • 1handful walnuts (chopped)

Instructions

  1. Cut the stracchino into small pieces
  2. Cook the penne according to package instructions
  3. While the pasta cooks, heat a pan over medium heat
  4. Add the milk, gorgonzola, and stracchino to the pan
  5. Stir continuously and melt the cheeses slowly into the milk — do this gently so the cheese does not burn
  6. When the pasta is done, reserve one cup of pasta water
  7. Drain the pasta and add it to the pan with the melted cheeses
  8. Add the mascarpone and about one-third of the reserved pasta water to the pan, stirring constantly to combine
  9. Remove the pan from heat and stir in the Pecorino Romano
  10. Mix thoroughly and sprinkle chopped walnuts over each served portion
