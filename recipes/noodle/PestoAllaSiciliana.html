Ingredients

  • 250g pasta
  • 8-10pcs cherry tomatoes
  • 20pcs almonds (peeled)
  • 10pcs basil leaves
  • 2cloves garlic
  • 20-30g pecorino (grated)
  • 40ml olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Instructions

  1. Toast the almonds lightly in a pan until they become fragrant
  2. Cook the pasta
  3. Blend the tomatoes, almonds, garlic, Pecorino, basil, olive oil, salt, and pepper into a sauce
  4. Reserve one cup of pasta water, then drain the rest
  5. Add the sauce to the pasta
  6. If the sauce is too thick, add a little pasta water
  7. Serve the pasta with Pecorino and fresh basil
