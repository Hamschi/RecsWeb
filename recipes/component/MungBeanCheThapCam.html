Ingredients

  • 100g mung bean
  • 0.3tsp salt
  • 400ml water
  • 100ml water (boiled)
  • 20g sugar
  • 5g vanilla sugar (alternatively 1 tsp vanilla extract)

Instructions

  1. Cook the mung beans
  2. Add the sugar, the vanilla sugar and the water to the mung beans and whisk so that you get a smooth paste
