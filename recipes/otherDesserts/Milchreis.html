Ingredients

  • 200g Milchreis
  • 800ml whole milk
  • 1pcs vanilla pod
  • 1pinch salt
  • 4tbsp sugar
  • cinnamon sugar

Instructions

  1. Pour the milk into a pot and add the sugar
  2. Flatten the vanilla pod, split it lengthwise, and scrape out the seeds
  3. Add both the vanilla seeds and the pod to the pot
  4. Bring the milk to a boil
  5. Slowly add the rice to the milk while stirring in a circular motion
  6. Stir briefly and add the salt
  7. Cover with a lid and let it simmer on low heat gently for 25–30 minutes
  8. Remove the vanilla pod
  9. Serve with cinnamon sugar
