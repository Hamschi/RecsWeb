Ingredients

  • 350ml coconut cream
  • 350ml whole milk
  • 50ml cream
  • 200ml water
  • 200g sugar
  • 1can lychees
  • 1can jackfruit
  • 1can palm's seeds in heavy sirup
  • pandan jelly
  • hạt lựu
  • 250g durian
  • ice cubes
  • Not included in Chè Thái: Mung bean or red beans
  • ice cubes

Instructions

  1. In a pot, heat the milk, cream, water, and sugar while stirring until fully dissolved
  2. Once the mixture has simmered for 20–30 seconds, add the coconut cream and dissolve it completely
  3. As soon as everything is combined, remove the pot from the heat and set aside
  4. To serve: add ice and toppings and pour the coconut mixture over the top
  5. Adding more toppings or switching toppings will make it to Chè Thập Cẩm
