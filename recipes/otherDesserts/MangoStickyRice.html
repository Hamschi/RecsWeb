Ingredients

  • 3.5cups rice
  • 3pcs pandan leaf
  • 250g sugar
  • 0.5tbsp salt
  • 300ml coconut milk
  • 200ml coconut milk
  • 1pinch salt
  • 1tsp corn starch
  • crispy mung bean (alternatively sesame)

Instructions

  1. Wash the glutinous rice and cook it
  2. Mix the sugar, 1/2 tbsp salt, and 300 ml coconut milk in a bowl
  3. Add the pandan leaves and transfer the mixture to a pot
  4. Cook the sauce over medium heat until the sugar has completely dissolved
  5. Remove the pandan leaves from the sauce
  6. Add the cooked sticky rice to the sauce and mix thoroughly
  7. Cover the pot and let it rest for 10 minutes
  8. Stir the rice thoroughly once more
  9. Cover again and let it rest for another 40 minutes
  10. Combine 200 ml coconut milk, salt, and cornstarch in a pot and cook over low to medium heat until thickened
  11. Cut the mango into pieces
  12. Serve the sticky rice with mango, drizzle with the coconut sauce, and top with crispy mung beans
