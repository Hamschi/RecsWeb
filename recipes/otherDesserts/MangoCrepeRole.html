Ingredients

  • 16g butter
  • 25g sugar
  • 2pcs eggs
  • 238g milk
  • 75g flour
  • 8g starch
  • 1tbsp vanilla extract
  • 220g whip cream
  • 20g sugar
  • 1pinch turmeric
  • 1pcs mango

Instructions

  1. Cut the mango into 1cm-cubes
  2. Melt the butter
  3. In a clean bowl, mix well melted butter, sugar
  4. Add the eggs and mix until you get a smooth texture
  5. Add and mix flour and starch to the batter
  6. Add and mix the milk and the vanilla extract to the batter until smooth
  7. Stir in the turmeric until the batter gets a light yellow color
  8. Sift the batter twice
  9. Heat a non-stick pan, rub butter onto a paper towel and use it to grease the pan
  10. Keep the heat low and let cook for about 1 minute or until you can easily peel off the crepe from the edges with the help of a spatula
  11. Repeat step 9-10 until you used up all the batter
  12. Let the crepes cool down on the cooling rack or on a plate
  13. Bring together heavy cream and sugar and use a hand mixer to beat until stiff peaks form
  14. Transfer into a piping bag. Set in the fridge to keep it cool
  15. Take three pieces of crepe and place them on the baking mat or pastry mat or parchment paper
  16. Spread on a layer of whipped cream and a layer of fresh mango cubes
  17. old in the edges and roll it up into a crepe roll
  18. Let set in the fridge to cool for 30 minutes
