Ingredients

  • 2pcs eggs
  • 125g sugar
  • 450g mascarpone
  • 300ml cream
  • a fewdrops lemon juice
  • coffee
  • 1splash Vin Santo (alternatively: liqueur, optional)
  • 200g ladyfingers
  • chocolate or cocoa powder

Instructions

  1. Separate the egg yolks from the whites and place them in separate bowls
  2. Add half of the sugar to the egg yolks and stir until smooth and evenly combined
  3. Fold the mascarpone into the egg yolk mixture
  4. Add the cream to the mascarpone mixture and mix until smooth
  5. Add the lemon juice to the egg whites and beat until stiff
  6. Gently fold the whipped egg whites into the mascarpone mixture
  7. Brew the coffee, mix it with the liqueur in a shallow dish, and dissolve the remaining sugar in it
  8. Spread a layer of mascarpone cream on the bottom of a dish
  9. Dip the ladyfingers briefly on both sides into the coffee and layer them over the mascarpone
  10. Continue layering mascarpone cream and coffee-soaked ladyfingers
  11. Finish by grating chocolate over the tiramisu or dusting it with cocoa powder
  12. Optional: let the tiramisu rest in the refridgerator for 3-4 hours, ideally over night
