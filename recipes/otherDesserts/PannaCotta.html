Ingredients

  • 375ml cream
  • 100g sugar
  • 3sheets gelatin
  • 40ml milk
  • 0.5pcs vanilla pod
  • 70g sugar
  • berry sauce

Instructions

  1. Soak the gelatin in cold water for 5 minutes
  2. Add the cream, milk, and sugar to a pot
  3. Scrape the vanilla seeds from the pod and add both seeds and pod to the pot
  4. Bring everything to a boil
  5. Once the gelatin has softened, squeeze out as much water as possible
  6. Remove the cream mixture from the heat and dissolve the gelatin in it
  7. Strain the mixture through a sieve into a bowl
  8. Let the mixture cool until it becomes slightly thickened
  9. Once cooled, pour into molds
  10. Refrigerate the panna cotta overnight
  11. Spread the berry sauce on a plate, unmold the panna cotta, and place it on top
  12. Tip: Remove the panna cotta from the fridge 20 minutes before serving.
