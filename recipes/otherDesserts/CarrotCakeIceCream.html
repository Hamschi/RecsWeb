Ingredients

  • 2tbsp butter
  • 4-5pcs carrots
  • 180g sugar (brown)
  • 0.5tsp salt
  • 1.25tsp cinnamon
  • 113g cream cheese
  • 400g sweetened condensed milk
  • 1tsp vanilla extract
  • 600ml heavy cream
  • 100g mixed nuts

Instructions

  1. Chop the mixed nuts and shred the carrots
  2. Combine butter, carrots, brown sugar, and salt in a saucepan over medium heat
  3. Cook and stir until carrots are just getting tender (about 5 minutes)
  4. Season with cinnamon and ginger and cook for 1 more minute
  5. Turn off the heat and let the mixture cool down
  6. Meanwhile, whisk together cream cheese, sweetened condensed milk, and vanilla extract in a bowl until smooth
  7. Stir in cooled carrot mixture and transfer to the refrigerator until needed
  8. Beat cream in a chilled glass or metal bowl with an electric mixer until medium-stiff peaks form
  9. Add cooled carrot-cream cheese mixture and whisk into the whipped cream
  10. Fold in chopped nuts with a spatula
  11. Transfer mixture into a chilled airtight container and place a piece of plastic wrap directly onto the mixture
  12. Cover with a lid and freeze until solid, at least 4 hours, but best overnight
