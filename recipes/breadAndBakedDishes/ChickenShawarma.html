Ingredients

  • 900g chicken (breast or tighs)
  • salt
  • 1tsp pepper
  • 1tbsp garlic powder
  • 1tbsp onion powder
  • 1tsp cumin
  • 1tsp turmeric
  • 1tsp red chili powder
  • 2tbsp tomato paste
  • 0.5pcs lemon
  • 3tbsp olive oil
  • 2pcs tomato
  • 0.5pcs cucumber
  • garlic toum
  • 2tbsp lemon juice
  • 1tbsp coriander (chopped)
  • 1pcs onion (red, large)
  • 1tbsp sumac
  • 8pcs pita bread

Instructions

  1. Cut the chicken into strips
  2. Marinate the chicken for 30 minutes with olive oil, tomato paste, lemon juice, garlic powder, onion powder, cumin, chili powder, turmeric, salt, and pepper
  3. Fry the chicken in a pan until fully cooked
  4. Meanwhile, cut the vegetables into strips
  5. Once the chicken is done, place the pita bread on top of the meat so it warms through and absorbs some of the juices
  6. Spread plenty of toum on the pita, then fill with chicken and vegetables
  7. Optional: finish with red onions, coriander, lemon juice, and/or sumac
