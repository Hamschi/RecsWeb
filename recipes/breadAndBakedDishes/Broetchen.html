Ingredients

  • 500g spelt flour type 630 (alternatively wheat flour type 550)
  • 300g cold water
  • 20g olive oil
  • 10g salt
  • 1g Hefe (frisch, alternativ 0.5g trocken)

Instructions

  1. Dissolve the yeast in the water
  2. Add the olive oil
  3. Mix the salt with the flour
  4. Add the flour to the liquid and mix until all the flour is fully incorporated
  5. Cover the dough and let it rest at room temperature overnight. The dough should roughly double in size
  6. Dust the dough and the work surface with a little flour
  7. Place the dough on the work surface and divide it into 9 pieces (about 90 g each)
  8. For each piece: pull the edges repeatedly toward the center, then shape into a roll. Repeat for all pieces
  9. Place the rolls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, cover them, and let them proof for 30–45 minutes
  10. Preheat the oven to 250°C (top and bottom heat)
  11. Place an oven-safe dish on the bottom rack
  12. Score the rolls with a serrated or sharp knife (about 2 mm deep)
  13. Mist the rolls with water
  14. Put the rolls into the oven and pour the 1/2 cup of hot water into the dish
  15. Bake for 20 minutes
  16. Let the rolls cool on a wire rack
