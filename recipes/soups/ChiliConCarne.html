Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 2pcs onions
  • 4cloves garlic
  • 1pcs chili
  • 1pcs thai chili
  • 2cans tomatoes (peeled)
  • 2small cans kidney beans
  • 300ml vegetable broth
  • 1.5tbsp tomato paste
  • 1stick cinnamon
  • 2tbsp paprika powder
  • 0.5tsp ground cumin
  • salt
  • pepper
  • corn (optional)

Instructions

  1. Finely dice the garlic and onions
  2. Sear the ground beef in a pot over high heat
  3. Once the meat is no longer pink and has dried off, add the tomato paste
  4. When the tomato paste has roasted, deglaze with a little vegetable broth to loosen the fond from the bottom of the pot
  5. Repeat the last step 2–3 times
  6. Stir in the paprika powder and cumin
  7. Add the onions and garlic
  8. Finely chop the chilies
  9. Once the onions turn translucent, sauté the chilies including the seeds
  10. Add the tomatoes, remaining vegetable broth, cinnamon stick, salt, and pepper
  11. Stir well and let simmer on low heat for 45 minutes
  12. Then stir in the kidney beans and let simmer for another 20 minutes
  13. Variations: use dark chocolate instead of cinnamon and/or add corn
